Inference is shifting. Lightweight models run locally for speed and privacy; heavier tasks still live in the cloud.
A hybrid pattern—edge pre-processing + cloud refinement—often wins.

## Decision Checklist
- **Latency budget**  
- **Privacy constraints**  
- **Battery/thermal limits**  
- **Unit economics**
