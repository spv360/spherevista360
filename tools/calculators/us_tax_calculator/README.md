# US Tax Calculator Suite

A comprehensive collection of tax calculation tools for US taxpayers, built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

## Available Calculators

### 1. Federal Income Tax Calculator
- Calculates federal income tax based on 2024 tax brackets
- Supports all filing statuses (Single, Married Joint, etc.)
- Includes standard deduction calculations
- Shows tax breakdown by bracket

### 2. State Income Tax Calculator
- Covers all 50 states plus DC
- Compares tax burdens across states
- Includes state-specific tax brackets and rates
- Shows state tax comparison grid

### 3. Capital Gains Tax Calculator
- Handles long-term and short-term capital gains
- Supports different asset types (stocks, real estate, collectibles)
- Includes cost basis adjustments
- Calculates net capital gains tax

### 4. Tax Withholding Calculator
- Analyzes current W-4 withholding
- Recommends optimal withholding amounts
- Considers multiple jobs and dependents
- Shows quarterly payment schedules

### 5. Self-Employment Tax Calculator
- Calculates Social Security and Medicare taxes
- Includes self-employment tax deduction
- Handles quarterly estimated payments
- Shows tax breakdown and payment schedule

### 6. Retirement Account Tax Calculator
- Covers Traditional IRA, Roth IRA, 401(k) accounts
- Calculates contribution tax benefits
- Estimates withdrawal taxes and penalties
- Includes RMD calculations

## Features

- **Responsive Design**: Works on desktop and mobile devices
- **Real-time Calculations**: Instant results as you type
- **Detailed Breakdowns**: Shows how taxes are calculated
- **Privacy First**: All calculations performed locally in browser
- **Current Tax Rates**: Based on 2024 IRS tax brackets and rates
- **Educational**: Includes explanations and disclaimers

## Usage

1. Access the suite at `/tools/calculators/us_tax_calculator/`
2. Choose a calculator from the main page
3. Enter your financial information
4. View detailed tax calculations and breakdowns

## Technical Details

- **Frontend**: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+)
- **Styling**: Modern gradient designs with hover effects
- **Validation**: Client-side input validation
- **No Dependencies**: Pure JavaScript, no external libraries required
- **Performance**: Optimized for fast calculations

## Disclaimer

These calculators provide estimates based on current tax laws and rates. They are not intended to provide tax advice. Please consult a qualified tax professional for personalized advice. Tax laws and rates may change.
